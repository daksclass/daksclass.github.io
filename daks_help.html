Buggy code?

No worries, bugs suck. We all get them in our code. Try to figure out what's up. Here are some troubleshooting steps:

  1. Figure out which line your problem is in. The error message and red x's can help you out with this.
  2. Check your semi colons, brackets, parenthesis, etc.
  3. Try commenting out bits of code to isolate your problem!
